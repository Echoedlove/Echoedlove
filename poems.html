Poems from My Heart

Welcome to my collection of heartfelt poems. These pieces are whispers of my soul, capturing moments of love, longing, and the beauty of quiet emotions. As you read, let these words carry you to a dreamy space where every line lingers like a soft, unspoken truth.

Drowning for You

He was the ocean,
I would drown in.
Iif it meant
He wouldn't be lonely.

Craving His Existence

I crave his existence like,
Love craves trust,
Sky craves stars in the darkness,
Sea craves shore that holds it close.

I crave his existence like,
Lovers crave their endless dove.

If only i knew

If only I knew, I'd yearn for those days,
Childhood's magic, in wonder-filled ways.
First glimpses of worlds, both far and wide,
Left me breathless, with a curious stride.

But now, in hindsight, I realize with a sigh,
If only I knew, I'd have cherished those times to the sky.
For in innocence, I roamed wild and free,
Unaware of life's complexities, yet to be.

Those carefree days, like falling leaves, slipped away,
Leaving memories, bittersweet, night and day.
If only I knew, I'd have held on tight,
To the enchantment of childhood's pure delight.

Symphony of Love

Oh love! Loving you was like
the ocean reaching for the shore,
the blooming of blossom trees,
warmth on a hibernal day.

Softly falling summer rain,
Gentle whispers in the night,
A haven from life's raging storms,
A guiding star that shone so bright.

Your love was a symphony,
That echoed through my soul,
A melody that made me whole,
Forever and always, my heart's goal.

The Paradox of Hope

In secret depths, I hide and ache
Yearning to escape, to break the chain
A glimmer of hope, a sunbeam bright
Lures me with warmth, and radiant light

But alas, the fire that I pursue
Becomes the flame that my soul subdues
A paradox of hope and despair
A heart trapped in shadows, beyond repair.

A Love Unspoken

He was never mine, I knew from the start
Yet I took his heart, and played with the art
Now I hold him tight, with a love so true
But the thought of losing him, haunts me anew
One day I'll let him go, and it will be my fate
The worst part of my life, an unbearable weight
I'll cherish every moment, every kiss and every sigh
For when he's gone, I'll be left to wonder why
But till then, I'll love him with all my might
And hold on to the memories, of our love so bright
Though it may not last, I'll make it forever true
For loving him, is all I can do."

A beautiful dream

"Life's fragile, every heartbeat matters
We never know when our last day arrives
So let's fill each moment with love and laughter
With those who make our hearts skip a beat, forever after
Let's cherish every smile, every tear, every sigh
And make each day a gift, to ourselves and those we love to fly
Simple moments, pure joy, that's all we need
To make life's journey, a beautiful dream"

Sanctuary of love

A home where love abides,
A haven where hearts reside.
She nurtures dreams with gentle care,
He provides with love and shares.

Together they build a life so true,
Filling emptiness with love anew.
Their home, a sanctuary of love,
Where memories are made, sent from above.
In every room, a memory's place,
Their love, a flame that time won't erase.
A beautiful home, where love does stay,
Fulfilling life, in its own sweet way.

The irony of love

"You beg and plead,
but still denied The things you crave,
forever left to hide But if you walk away,
with heart aflame His new love will claim,
the very things you couldn't obtain
The irony of love,
a bitter pill to swallow What you begged for,
will be hers to follow So leave with pride,
and don't look back For in his eyes,
you'll see your own heart's attack"

A World of Our Own

In nature’s arms, where love once bloomed,
I wander free, no longer doomed.
The skies above, the earth below,
Whisper your name, where soft winds blow.

Though you’re not here, you still remain,
In every leaf, in every rain.
The world around me, so pure and wide,
Reflects the love I feel inside.

I live in dreams, a fairytale spun,
Where our love story’s never undone.
No worries, no fears, just endless light,
In our world, everything feels right.

You walk a path where others tread,
But in my heart, you’re still ahead.
Let them speak, let them say their words,
For only ours are what’s truly heard.

In a world where change is always near,
Where hearts may fade, and people disappear,
I’ll stand by you, unwavering, true—
A love so strong, it breaks through.

Let’s build a world where we both belong,
Where no one’s words can feel too strong.
A place where time is never cruel,
Where love, like ours, makes its own rule.

So, let them change, let them grow,
In our world, we’ll always know:
I’m yours, you’re mine, no matter the cost,
In a love where nothing’s ever lost.

Tied by Fate

In the vast expanse of the endless sky,
Where galaxies swirl and stars drift by,
We found each other, lost yet found,
In the rhythm of time, where fate is crowned.

Dimensions wide, in endless space,
A twist of destiny, we embraced.
How many paths could we have walked,
Yet here we are, as the universe talked.

Fate wove its thread with careful hands
A masterpiece in celestial sands.
So many forces, unseen, untold,
Guiding us through the stories of old.

You and I, a moment so rare,
A meeting meant to be, beyond compare.
Who are we to question this plan,
When stars themselves decreed our span?

For in this life, as worlds collide,
We stand as one, side by side.
No force, no hand, no voice can sever,
What fate has bound, what love will treasure forever.

Sacrifice for Love

Oh, fate, my guide, my heart, my soul,
Take my being, make me whole.
I’ll offer all, without a trace,
If only you’d grant me his embrace.

Take my body, take my mind,
Let me leave the world behind.
But give me back the love I crave,
His essence, his heart, forever to save.

I'll walk through flames, endure the night,
Cross shattered glass, with no respite.
Each step a sacrifice, each breath a plea,
All for the chance to have him with me.

If his old self could be returned,
With every lesson, every year learned,
I’d lay it all down, no fear, no strife,
To breathe again in the warmth of his life.

Let me be the one who bears the cost,
For in his love, I find no loss.
I’ll sacrifice the world to make him see,
That forever, he and I are meant to be.

A Dream of Home

When I was young, I dreamt of more,
Of careers and titles to adore.
But now as time begins to sway,
My heart desires a different way.

A home, a haven, soft and bright,
Where love blooms gently in the light.
No chaos, no loud voices near,
Just peaceful days, with hearts sincere.

I dream of moments, calm and true,
Where laughter echoes, soft as dew.
Of cooking meals with gentle hands,
And grocery trips with simple plans.

A family, loving, kind, and strong,
Where no one’s right, and no one’s wrong.
Where peace is found in every part,
And every day feels like a start.

So I wish for this, my quiet plea,
A home where love is all I see
No grand success, no shining fame
Just hearts entwined, forever the same.

A Reflection of Change

Once a child, so pure, so free,
Dancing through life, no cares to see.
The sweetest smile, the brightest glow,
A heart unscarred, with love to show.

She didn’t know what the future held,
The storms, the doubts, where they’d be felled.
The world so vast, yet she stood tall,
Unaware of the shadows that would come to call.

With innocence, she loved so deep,
Expecting the world would love her to keep.
But life’s not sugar, it’s not always kind,
And she learned that truth as time unwind.

Insecurities crept, doubts took their place,
A quiet reflection, a longing for grace.
She compared, she questioned, what went wrong,
When all she wanted was to belong.

But the world, she’ll learn, is not a fair game,
It takes more than love to rise to fame.
She’ll grow, she’ll fall, and she’ll rise again,
For strength comes through the deepest pain.

So, little one, don’t mourn the change,
For in the end, it’s all in range.
The sweetest girl, the brightest light,
Can still shine through the darkest night.

Afraid of Losing You

You are mine, and I am yours,
A love so pure, it forever endures.
In seeking you, I lost my way,
Yet found a love that will always stay.

But in the quiet of my heart,
A fear resides, a tear apart.
What if this world, so harsh, so cold,
Finds ways to sever what we hold?

You are the one I can't bear to lose,
The light in my life, the warmth I choose.
And yet, the world with all its might,
Could steal you from me in the dead of night.

What if they try, with cruel hands,
To tear apart our distant lands?
My soul shivers at the thought alone,
For in your love, I've found my home.

But still, my love, we’ll stand as one,
With hearts united, till time is done.
No force, no world, no raging tide,
Can ever break what we’ve inside.
