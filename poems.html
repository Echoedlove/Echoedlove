Poems from My Heart

Welcome to my collection of heartfelt poems. These pieces are whispers of my soul, capturing moments of love, longing, and the beauty of quiet emotions. As you read, let these words carry you to a dreamy space where every line lingers like a soft, unspoken truth.

Drowning for You

My thoughts of you are endless,
A storm that never calms.
I drown in the beauty of your smile,
And the peace of your calm.

I reach for you in the midnight sky,
As dreams pull me under.
Drowning for you in every heartbeat,
You are my thunder.

For every word you never spoke,
I loved you even more.
Lost in your eyes, the silent love,
Is the only thing I adore.

Craving His Existence

I crave his existence like,
Love craves trust,
Sky craves stars in the darkness,
Sea craves shore that holds it close.

I crave his existence like,
Lovers crave their endless dove.

Falling Through Silence

In the silence, I fall,
Tumbling without sound,
The world spins around me,
But I am lost, unbound.

I search for your presence,
In the air, in the sky,
But you're nowhere to be found,
Just the echo of my sigh.

Unwritten Letters

I wrote you letters without ink,
On pages that never turned,
Each word carved in silence,
Yet the yearning still burned.

The paper never met the pen,
But in my heart, you’ll always stay,
Unwritten, unspoken, yet forever true,
In a world of words, just for you.

Stardust Dreams

I dream of stardust falling,
Of skies that never end,
Of endless love, that whispers
In the arms of the stars, my friend.

You are the dream I chase at night,
The star that guides my way,
In every dream, in every breath,
You are the light that stays.

The Silent Symphony

In the silence, there’s a song,
Unheard, but felt inside,
A melody so sweet and soft,
That I can’t help but hide.

It plays within my heart each day,
And every note is you,
A symphony of love so pure,
And in silence, I hear it true.

Waves of You

Like waves against the shore,
I am pulled toward you evermore.
With every rush of water’s might,
I am lost in your endless light.

Though I may drown in your deep embrace,
I’ll never let go of your grace.

Timeless Moments

Time stands still when I’m with you,
Every second feels like years,
We are timeless in our love,
Erasing all doubts and fears.

Our moments are the echoes of eternity,
And in your eyes, I see the infinite.

The Last Goodbye

You whispered goodbye so softly,
Like a breeze that touched my soul,
Yet your absence left a hole,
A space that swallows me whole.

But in that goodbye, I found my truth,
That love never truly leaves.
It lingers in the quiet moments,
In the memories we grieve.

Whispers of a Dream

Your whispers fill my dreams,
Soft and tender, full of grace,
They carry me to places,
I can see your loving face.

In my sleep, I hear your voice,
And it’s enough to make me rejoice.

Rays of Hope

I hold onto the rays of hope,
That shine through every storm,
They keep me warm when the cold sets in,
And guide me to where love is born.

Through every dark and lonely night,
Hope brings me to the light.

The Sweetest Sigh

In the quiet of the night,
I let out a sigh so sweet,
For in this moment, you’re with me,
In dreams, we’ll always meet.

That sigh carries my love for you,
Soft, but deep, and ever true.

My Silent Prayer

In the silence, I pray for you,
For all the things you dream,
That love will find a way,
And you’ll always be serene.

My prayer is not spoken, yet it’s loud,
For you’re my heart’s eternal vow.

In Your Eyes

In your eyes, I find my world,
A universe that’s endless,
A space where love unfurls,
And every moment’s boundless.

In your gaze, I see the stars,
And in your smile, I find my home.

The Space Between

There’s a space between us,
So vast and so wide,
But in every heart’s whisper,
I can feel you by my side.

It’s a space that holds our love,
Even if we’re far apart,
For we are together, always,
In the silence of the heart.
